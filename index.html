Common dilemmas

Questions founders ask before investing in Google Ads

SEO brings free traffic, so why do I need PPC?

SEO is valuable, but PPC helps you capture active demand faster, test offers, learn keyword intent, and enter new markets while organic visibility builds.

What if I already spent money on Google Ads without results?

That usually means the account, search terms, landing page, tracking, or offer-message match needs review. The first step is diagnosing the leakage before increasing spend.

Is this only for big brands?

No. It is for businesses with enough budget and operational readiness to test properly, measure clearly, and follow up with leads.

Can you help with landing pages too?

Yes. Landing page development is one of CMC’s best-selling services and is especially useful when PPC traffic is relevant but conversions are weak.
